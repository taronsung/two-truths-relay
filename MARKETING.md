### r/webdev / r/reactjs

**Title:** I built a viral social game in a weekend - Two Truths Relay (Next.js + Framer Motion)

**Body:**
Hey everyone! I just shipped Two Truths Relay - a chain-reaction social bluff game that transforms the classic "2 truths and a lie" into something viral.

**How it works:**
1. Someone sends you a link with 3 statements
2. You guess which one is the lie
3. You add YOUR own 2 truths + 1 lie
4. Share with friends to continue the relay
5. Watch the tree grow as friends branch off!

**Tech stack:**
- Next.js 16 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Framer Motion for animations
- Supabase-ready schema

**What I'm proud of:**
- The cozy pastel design system
- Buttery smooth animations (200-500ms micro-interactions)
- Natural viral loop built into the core experience
- Works great on mobile

Live demo: https://dlrp9n-3000.csb.app/

**Tagline:** The viral social bluff game that grows like a tree

**Description:**
Two Truths Relay transforms the classic party game into a viral chain reaction. Spot the lie, add your own 2 truths + 1 lie, share with friends, and watch a beautiful tree visualization grow as the relay spreads through your network.

**Key Features:**
- Instant fun - playable in 30 seconds
- Natural virality - sharing is built into the core loop
- Beautiful design - cozy pastel colors, smooth animations
- Tree visualization - see how far the relay spreads
- No login required - just open and play
- Open source - deploy your own instance

**Makers Quote:**
"I wanted to create a game that's genuinely fun AND naturally viral. By making sharing a core part of the gameplay (not just a share button), every player becomes a distributor."
